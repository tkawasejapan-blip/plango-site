Privacy Policy for PlanGo

Last updated: May 20, 2026

PlanGo ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

PlanGo does not require user registration or login.

The App may collect limited information necessary to provide its functionality:

  • Selected cities or destinations
  • App usage data
  • Device information
  • Approximate location (only if permission is granted)

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or payment details.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for:

  • Generating travel itineraries
  • Improving route accuracy
  • App performance and stability
  • Technical troubleshooting

3. Google Maps

PlanGo uses Google Maps services for route display and travel time estimation.

Use of Google Maps is subject to Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps Platform Terms of Service

4. Server Communication

The App communicates with backend servers to generate travel plans and retrieve sightseeing information.

Server logs may temporarily include:

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Request timestamps

This data is used only for service operation, maintenance, and security purposes.

5. Data Sharing

PlanGo does not sell personal data.

Data is not shared with third parties except when required for:

  • Google Maps functionality
  • Legal compliance
  • Security or fraud prevention

6. Data Retention

Technical logs may be stored temporarily and are periodically deleted when no longer needed.

7. Children's Privacy

PlanGo is not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
