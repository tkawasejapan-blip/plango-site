# Privacy Policy for PlanGo Last updated: May 20, 2026 PlanGo ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the App and how it is used. ## 1. Information We Collect PlanGo does not require user registration. The App may collect limited technical information necessary to provide travel planning features, including: - Selected cities or travel destinations - App usage information - Device information - Approximate location information (only if permission is granted) No personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or payment information is collected. ## 2. How Information Is Used Collected information is used only for: - Generating sightseeing itineraries - Improving route accuracy - Operating and improving the App - Troubleshooting technical issues ## 3. Google Maps PlanGo uses Google Maps services. Use of Google Maps is subject to Google's: - Google Privacy Policy - Google Maps Platform Terms of Service Google may collect and process data according to its own policies. Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy ## 4. Server Communication The App communicates with backend servers to retrieve sightseeing information and generate travel plans. Server logs may temporarily contain technical information such as: - IP address - Device type - Request timestamps This information is used only for service operation and security purposes. ## 5. Data Sharing PlanGo does not sell personal information. Information is not shared with third parties except when required for: - Google Maps functionality - Legal compliance - Security and fraud prevention ## 6. Data Retention Technical logs may be retained temporarily for operational and security purposes and are periodically deleted. ## 7. Children's Privacy PlanGo is not directed to children under 13. ## 8. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page. ## 9.
