Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is PlanGo?

PlanGo is a travel planning app that generates realistic one-day sightseeing itineraries based on selected cities. It helps users plan efficient and practical travel routes.

2. Does PlanGo require an account or login?

No. PlanGo does not require any user registration, login, or account creation.

3. Does PlanGo include in-app purchases or subscriptions?

No. PlanGo is completely free and does not include any in-app purchases or subscriptions.

4. Does PlanGo collect personal data?

PlanGo does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or payment information.

5. Does PlanGo use my location?

Location data is only used if you grant permission. It is used to help generate more relevant travel plans and routes.

6. What map service does PlanGo use?

PlanGo uses Google Maps to display routes and estimate travel times.

7. Does PlanGo store my data?

PlanGo may temporarily process technical data such as device type, request time, and IP address for service operation. This data is not used to identify users and is deleted periodically.

8. Why does PlanGo need a server?

The server is used to generate travel itineraries and retrieve sightseeing information. It helps ensure accurate and up-to-date travel planning results.

9. How do I report a problem or bug?

Please contact us via email with details of the issue, including device model and steps to reproduce the problem.
